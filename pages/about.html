MY JOURNEY

Connecting the dots from where I've been to what I build.

Scene 1: The Origin

My journey started in Los Angeles, CA. Growing up in such a diverse and dynamic hub sparked my curiosity for how complex systems work and how data connects everything.

Scene 2: New Horizons

That curiosity led me to Austin, TX, a city buzzing with technology and innovation. This is where I truly began to focus my passion for data into a career.

Scene 3: The Proving Ground

With experience providing data solutions for diverse industries, I joined the Golden State Warriors. Here, I design, build, and maintain data infrastructure that powers machine learning pipelines for predictive analytics products focused on athlete performance and coaching.

I get to solve complex problems that require my expertise in enabling and automating end-to-end data workflows, translating complex technical data into actionable insights for non-technical stakeholders.

Scene 4: My Toolkit

Currently, my focus is on pipelines & models for basketball analytics. My primary tools for building robust, scalable solutions include:

  • SQL
  • dbt
  • Airflow
  • Python
  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP)
